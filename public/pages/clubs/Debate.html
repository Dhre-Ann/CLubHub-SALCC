Welcome to the vibrant world of debate at the Sir Arthur Lewis Community College (SALCC) !

Our Debate Club is a dynamic and engaging platform that provides students with the opportunity to hone their critical thinking, public speaking, and argumentation skills in a supportive and intellectually stimulating environment.

What sets our Debate Club apart is our commitment to addressing a diverse range of topics, from global issues to local concerns. Through structured debates, impromptu speaking sessions, and mock trials, members gain a comprehensive understanding of various subjects while refining their ability to think on their feet.

Join us on this exciting journey of self-discovery, where your voice matters, your ideas flourish, and your confidence soars. Embrace the art of debate at SALCC and unlock the potential to become a persuasive, informed, and articulate communicator.
